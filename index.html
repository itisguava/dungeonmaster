How to Play Dungeon Master

Welcome, Adventurer!

Dungeon Master is a weapon crafting RPG. Forge weapons, enchant them, gear up your character, and dive into a 20+ floor dungeon crawler.

Game Loop:
1. Forge a weapon (choose metal, length, play mini-games)
2. Quench it for bonus durability
3. Enchant it by spinning the wheel
4. Save it to your inventory & equip it
5. Enter the Dungeon and fight your way through floors
6. Level up, unlock skills, and prestige for permanent bonuses

Forging Weapons

Forge Tab — Where you craft weapons.

1. Choose your base: Weapon type, metal, blade length, hilt, and technique. Better materials = better stats, but they cost gold or dungeon materials.

2. Play mini-games:
• Metal Spin — Stop the wheel in the green zone
• Reaction Test — Click when the circle turns green
• Rhythm Forge — Press Space when the beat hits the target zone

3. Design your weapon: Customize the blade shape, edge style, engravings, and curve.

4. Quench: Stop the cursor in the sweet spot for bonus durability.

5. Save: Click "Save Current Sword" to add it to your inventory.

Enchanting & Shopping

Enchant Tab — Spin the wheel (costs 25 gold) to add a random enchantment to your sword. Rarer enchantments have smaller slices. Prestige increases your luck!

Enchantment Effects in Dungeon:
• Fire = flame particles • Ice = slows enemies • Lightning = chains to nearby enemies
• Poison = damage over time • Wind = knockback • Explosive = AOE on kill

Shop Tab — Buy material unlocks and sell swords. Better metals and hilts need to be unlocked here first.

Dungeon Crawler

Controls:
WASD / Arrow Keys — Move
Space — Attack nearest enemy
R — Dodge roll (direction you're facing)
1, 2, 3, 4 — Use equipped abilities
Walk onto stairs + S — Go to next floor
Tips:
• Equip a sword before entering
• Enemies chase you when close — attack with Space
• Combo hits for bonus damage (hit quickly in succession)
• Find chests for loot and gold
• NPCs (merchant/healer) appear in rooms — walk into them
• Purple glowing icons are events — walk into them for choices
• Cracked walls can be attacked to reveal secret rooms
• Boss fights on floors 5, 10, 15, and 20
• Enable Mobile Controls in Settings for touch play

Abilities & Classes

Abilities Tab — Unlock abilities with gold, equip up to 4. Press 1-4 in the dungeon to use them. Mana regens as you walk.

Classes (Character Tab):
• Warrior — +20% HP, +10% DMG, heal on kill
• Rogue — +25% crit, faster dodge, +20% speed
• Mage — +40% mana, 2x mana regen, +15% ability DMG
• Paladin — passive heal, +15% defense, holy smite every 5th hit

Skill Tree (Skills Tab) — Spend skill points (earned by leveling up) on passive buffs across Combat, Defense, and Utility branches.

Progression & Endgame

XP & Levels — Earn XP from kills, forging, and dungeon clears. Each level gives +1 skill point.

Prestige — Reset progress for permanent +5% gold and +3% enchant luck per level. Gets harder each time.

Ascension — Every 10 prestiges triggers an Ascension: massive permanent bonuses (+DMG, +HP, +Gold, +XP).

Endless Mode — Toggle in Dungeon tab. After floor 20, the dungeon keeps going with infinitely scaling enemies.

Challenge Modes — Glass Cannon (1HP/5x DMG), Speed Run, No Abilities, Boss Rush, Ironman.

Achievements — Unlock titles, skill points, and auras from hard achievements.
